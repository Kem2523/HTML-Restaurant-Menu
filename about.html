Origin Story

The owner first opened up H-Thai-ML in Milwaukee, WI. H-Thai-ML has been open for over 15 years and quickly became a local staple. H-Thai-ML continues to grow and has opened up three other locations in the US. We love giving great service and providing our customers with delicious food. Stop in and give us a try!
